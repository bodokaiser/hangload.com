September 2014 ADFC Lüneburg - Hangload presented as alternative bag holder for bikes

The ADFC Lüneburg present various possibilities how to carry load on your bike. Hangload is mentioned as a new alternative - for avoiding the danger of going by bike with shopping bags on your handle bars.

ADFC Lüneburg : carrying daily loads on your bike



May 2014 Company Formation Hangload UG (haftungsbeschränkt)

Since the middle of May 2014 Hangload is a registered Limited (UG) in Germany.
Since then we have established local production and are now starting to bring Hangload to the market and on your bikes.



March 2014 Blog Europeancycling presents Hangload

In the blog Hangload is presented as a new concept for carrying heavy shopping bags and rucksacks without any danger with your bike. Above all, the possible usage of various bag shapes and sizes is mentioned.
