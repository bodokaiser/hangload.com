How it can work for you

The Hangload is a new easy and practical way of carrying various bags on a bicycle with a standard rear rack.

A toggle with band attaches to the top of the bicycle rear rack and an angled-out panel attaches to the side of the rear rack.

Bags of various shapes and sizes can be hung by their handle onto the toggle and then placed to rest on the angled-out panel on the side of the rack. Stainless steel banner Hangloads can be fitted onto either or both sides of your bicycle rack.
