Benefits and what it makes different.

No Insecurities

Did you sometime went home from shopping and just put your shopping bag on the handlebar? How did this feel? Did you had problems keeping the balance? I guess you had. By beeing fixed to the rear wheel there is less chance of the Hangload causing you insecurities while cycling your bags from A to B.

No Sweat Stains

It is a sunny day. You got your BagJack bagpack on the back start cylcing to your destination. Your a bit late and while being in action it is getting hot and hotter. Finally you reach your rendevous but your shirt is full of sweat. With the Hangload you easily can mount your bagpack on the rear wheel and your upper body is completly cooled by fresh air.

No Repacking

We love traditional bicycle bags. However they can get exhausting in terms of repacking. Went shopping? Then move all items from the shopping bag into the fixed bicycle bag. Arrived at home? Then move all items from the bicycle bag to another bag to get upstairs. Hangload frees you from your selected transport medium. Shopping bag, rucksack, whatever as long as there are sleeves to mount to the toggle.

No Discomfort

Transporting load with your bicycle previously was always an exchange with comfort. Regarding of the bag caused discomfort by its weight or by the position it was mounted to. You always were forced to keep an eye on your medium and watch out that you do not strike it for example with your feet while cycling. Hangload minimizes this problem by shifting your bag into a 45 degree angle which creates space for your feet.
