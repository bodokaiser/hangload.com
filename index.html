The new way
to load your bike

—Created in Berlin—

The Hangload is a new and practical way
of carrying various bags on a bicycle
with any standard bike rack.

The fixed-angled Hangload can be fitted
onto either one or both sides of your bicycle rack.

Bags of various shapes and sizes can be hung
by their handle onto the toggle,
then placed to rest on the angled-out panel.

One tool, many benefits

Versatility

Carry all your bags, from simple shopping bags to big bagpacks. Use your bike for everything!

Simplicity

Mount it on your bike in literally 30 seconds.

Safety

Enjoy a better bicycle control and avoid carrying bags on your bike's handle bars.

Healthiness

Take the load off your back---avoid back pain or unsightly sweat marks.
