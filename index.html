The new way to load your bicycle.

Workings What it is.

General

The Hangload is a new way of how to carry load on the bicycle. It consists out of two components: A toggle where you can hang your bag on to and a panel which pushes the bag from the wheels.

Toggle

The toggle is a piece of wood with a textile band and two D-rings. The wood is the place where you can put the sleeve of your bag on to whereas the textile band with the D-rings are there to mount the toggle on the bicycle racks. To attach the toggle you neither need a screw driver nor any specific clip - you actually not even need to do a knot!

Panel

The panel task pushes the bag away from the wheel and stops the bag from swinging around and getting into the gear.

Benefits How it is different.

Low-cost

The Hangload was constructed to be a low-cost product. It should be achievable by anyone to a fair price while still providing a high quality and sustainable materials.

Large payload

The Hangload can carry a lot of payload depending on the bag you use. We already carried 10 kilogramms of metal with it or big delivery packages. The Hangload did not complain and done its job well.

Safe and stable control

Every thing you hang on the Hangload is hold by the toggle which is attached to the bicycle rack. As the bicycle rack is a central part of the bicycle it will distribute all weight equally on the rear wheel. So you only feel small weight on the side and you can maintain a safe and stable bike control.

Works with different bags

As the Hangload only provides you the baggage system you are not limited to a specific type of bag. From shopping bags to rucksacks every bag which has a sleeve for the toggle fits on to the Hangload.
