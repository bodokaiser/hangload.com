Lean & simple

Easily install your Hangload to any standard bike rack. No tools needed, no moving parts. Once set up, the theft-proof Hangload simply stays on your bike.

Learn more

Carry more

Hangload's safe and durable system allows you to carry almost anything on your bike— grocery bags, backpacks, or suitcases, even crates.

Learn more

A safer ride

The Hangload’s sturdy steel frame keeps your hands free and your luggage at a safe distance to your bike’s spokes and pedals. No more dangling bags from the handle bar.
