Hangload Banner

The fixed-angled Hangload Banner can be fitted onto either or both sides of your bicycle rack. Banners available in a choice of colors.

How it works

See the benefits

The Hangload Banner a new easy and practical way of carrying various bags on a bicycle with a standard rear rack.

A toggle with band attaches to the top of the bicycle rear rack and an angled-out panel attaches to the side of the rear rack.

Bags of various shapes and sizes can be hung by their handle onto the toggle and then placed to rest on the angled-out panel on the

side of the rack. Hangload Banners can be fitted onto either or both sides of your bicycle rack.



Benefits

    Carries many types of bags easily

    shopping bags - rucksacks - sport bags - ladies bags - laptop bags - travel bags etc. ...



  • Simple to use

  • Carries various shapes and sizes

  • Inexpensive

  • Safe and stable bicycle control - carrying bags on the bikes handle bars can be dangerous

  • Prevents bags from coming into contact with bicycle

  • Restrains bags from swinging

  • Takes the load off your back - no back pain or unsightly sweat marks

  • Enables unrestricted pedalling even when carrying larger bags

  • Keeps bags clean and away from muddy wheel splashes

  • When Hangload Banner is fixed to the bicycle rack, cyclists can carry loads more and be spontaneous
