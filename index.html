How it can work for you

The Hangload is a new easy and practical way of carrying various bags on a bicycle.

A toggle with band attaches to the top of the bicycle rear rack and an angled-out panel attaches to the side of the rear rack.

Bags of various shapes and sizes can be hung by their handle onto the toggle and then placed to rest on the angled-out panel on the side of the rack. Of course it is possible to have Hangload panels on both sides of your bicycle.

Freedom

The Hangload really opens up the possibilities of what can easily be transported in bags on bikes.

The angled-out panels push the load outward away from the bicycle and so restrict bag swinging while protecting the bicycles components and enabling ample room to peddle, even when carrying larger bags!
The angled panel keeps the bag clean and away from bicycle wheel splashes. Also the panel prevents bicycle and bags coming into contact while riding.

Hangload is ideal when using for shopping, cycling to work or to sports, for picnics and bike touring. Some have used it to carry photographic equipment and other have carried large picture frames even chairs and small BBQs in large bags.

Shopping bags, rucksacks, sport bags, travel bags, infact most bags with a handle can be carried on a Hangload.

Payload

Hangload allows you to carry bags of various shapes and sizes and even impressively heavy bags can be hung from its strong toggle and band to the rest on its robust panels.

You can reach the maximum capacity that your bicycle rack is designed to carry!

Typical container carriers hold the load from underneath and only carry loads that can fit into their constructed shape, whereas Hangload enables cyclists to carry a much larger variety of shapes, sizes and weights safely and comfortably.
They look pretty cool too and can be left fixed to the bicycle carrier.

Construction

The Hangload concept consists of many new and unique features, which are designed to create a low-cost but high quality product.

Due to its unique mechanics of hanging a load and resting it at an angle with easy to replace components, it is even possible to produce the Hangload system at a modest cost.

All components are from Germany and the Hangload is produced locally in Berlin.
