Attachment of the Hangload PE retractable on your bike

Above all the back bungee cord must be tight. For more detailled information, please see the video under this section.



video instructions how to attach the PE retractable version

After fixing it should be ensured that the rear rubber band is really tight. If this is not the case, it helps to make one or two nodes in the band. Then the rubber band is shorter overall and is again tight.
