Techniques, tips and tricks about Hangload



We try to continually to improve our product. There are however, some techniques and tricks, as the functionality and manner of use of your Hangload gets easier and safer for you.

Please write us at info@hangload.com , if you have improvements or other tips for users.

Have fun with your Hangload!








Bags with long handles (longer than 17 cm )


Some bags, especially shopping bags or some women handbags, have very long handles. If you would hang this on your Hangload the bag dangling by the long handles and possibly interfere with pedaling. To avoid this, wrap your bag handle around your rear rack and Hangload toggle just as shown on the video and picture underneath.
