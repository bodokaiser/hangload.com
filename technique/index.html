Techniques, tipps and tricks about Hangload



We try to continually improve our product. There are however, some techniques and tricks, as the functionality and manner of use of your Hangload gets easier and safer for you.

Please write us at info@hangload.com , if you have improvements or other tipps for users.

Have fun with your Hangload!








Bags with long handles (longer than 17 cm )


Some bags, especially cloth shopping bags or some women handbags, have very long handles. If you would hang this on your Hangload the bag dangling by the long handles and possibly interfere with pedaling.
To avoid this, wrap the bag handle just a few times around the Hangload toggle that is attached to your carrier. By shortening the bag is stable and secure on the panel of the Hangload and you can safely continue riding your bike.



Use banner as bag


We now have already received some feedback and heard from some that they would like to use the banner of the stainless steel version as a bag to store eg Newspapers or the bike lock there.
Newspapers or thicker magazines you can just plug in the banner without falling out. However, in order to ensure this, we recommend that the Hangload banner is sewed up to glued or use a staple.
It is essential that the banner can no longer be changed easily. Only in the unchanged state, you can change your banner as you like.



Boxcut version


In developing the Hangload to the finished product today, we have developed some craft versions like this cardboard version.
The cardboard replaces the panel, bungee cords and toggle are needed as before.
