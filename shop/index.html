Hangload Banner

The fixed-angled Hangload Banner can be mounted on either side of your carrier—optionally on both sides simultaneously. Available in grey and green.

Features

  • Frame: 100% stainless steel with perbunan tubing.
  • Textile: nylon (80% nylon, 20% polyurethane) in grey and green.
  • Attachment: bungee cords made of polypropylene toggle.
  • 100% beech wood.
  • D-Rings made of stainless steel.
  • Webbing band in polyacryl.

Specs

  • Width: 36 cm
  • Depth: 12 cm
  • Weight: 536 g
