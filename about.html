About Hangload

Hangload provides a new and patented system to load different bag sizes and shapes on your bike. Individal adjustments of size or color of the banner can meet the costumer requirements.
It was the idea from the beginning, to produce a Hangload as cheap as possible, but with high quality, environmentally friendly and with local production.
Hangload was designed, that the costumer can easily replace damaged or lost parts by himself.
Hangload wants to offer the possibility to load all sorts of formats of bags (as long as they have a handle) on your bike - affordable for everyone.

Team

Darius Hajiani

Darius Hajiani moved to Berlin in early 2004. In Berlin he was amazed by the ability to live without the car. However he always felt uncomfortable carrying load with the bicycle and he wondered why there is no device which properly handles different types of bags without being to expensive or limited to specific fixed bags. So he started working on the Hangload and got the best solution which fits the needs of the customers, the environment and local businesses.
