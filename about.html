Team Who stands behind Hangload?

Darius Hajiani Inventor and Founder

Darius Hajiani is the son of an iranian father and an english mother. He moved to Berlin in early 2004 because of his daughter. In Berlin he was amazed by the ability to live without the car and this was the first time in his life when he did not own a car anymore. However he always felt uncomfortable carrying load with the bicycle and he wondered why there is no device which properly handles different types of bags without being to expensive or limited to specific fixed bags. So he started working on the Hangload and after seven years he finally sorted out all major problems and got the best solution which fits the needs of the customers, the environment and local businesses. His dream is to make the Hangload public to all people and make the world a better place by having access to the Hangload as useful helper for any load you want to carry per bike.

Bodo Kaiser Marketing and Distribution

Bodo Kaiser is a young local which is in to the idea of having an own business giving benefits to the society by the age of 15 where he started building computers and servicing local businesses with their computer needs. After being quite successful in this area he moved over to web development. With the age of 18 he founded satisfeet an ecommerce which offers fair socks to a fair price. He believes in the Hangload and expects to be a good fit for distributing the Hangload online.

Matthew Pentycofe Social Media

Originally hailing from San Francisco where he studied music business, Matthew moved to Berlin in the summer of 2013. He began his career in social media in San Francisco as an intern, where he was in charge of social media at two renowned record labels. Upon moving to Berlin he has continued to work in social media in the music and entertainment industry. As Berlin is a biking city Matthew found a new love for riding a bicycle as a means of transportation and through his newfound love began to branch his social media experience out past the music industry. He is happy and excited to be a part of the Hangload team to explore this new medium.
