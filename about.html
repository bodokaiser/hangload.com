About Hangload

Hangload is a patented system that is designed to carry bags of various shapes and sizes on the rear rack of bicycles.

Shopping bags, rucksacks, sport bags, travel bags, in fact most bags with a handle can be carried on a Hangload.

It has always been our intention to produce a useful, stylish and inexpensive product to give cyclists a new option for carrying various sized bags and so making it even more convenient to go by bike!

The whole principle is based on simplicity and practicality using the most suitable means to ensure a high performance and quality at a low cost.
