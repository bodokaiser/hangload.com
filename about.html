About Hangload

Hangload is a patented system that is designed to carry conventional bags of various shapes and sizes on the rear rack of bicycles. Shopping bags, rucksacks, sport bags, travel bags, in fact most bags with a handle can be carried on a Hangload.
It has always been our intention to produce a useful, stylish and inexpensive product to give cyclists a new option for carrying various sized bags and so making it even more convenient to go by bike!
The whole principle is based on simplicity and practicality using the most suitable means to ensure a high performance and quality at a low cost.

Team

Darius Hajiani

Settled in Berlin in 2007. For the first time in his life he was impressed by how in a city he could comfortably get around by bike and did not require a car. He very much felt the pleasure and benefits of using his bicycle on a daily basis. The only drawback for him was that he could not find a suitable carrying device that enabled him to get his things about without fuss or a huge expense and fear of theft. He started thinking...
After much prototyping with many cardboard boxes over a period of time he came up with a solution that worked very well. He learnt metal work and much about textile sewing and looking into the most ecological plastic for this purpose and so the Hangload was developed.
He uses his every day whether shopping, transporting all types of work materials, going for a picknick or even getting his heavy rucksack around town. Developing the Hangload would not have been possible without a Hangload!

Maria Slaby is a young woman living in Berlin who is interested in nature, sustainability and new ways of living in a city.
