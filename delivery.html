Delivery

Although these prices are guaranteed at time of publication and once we receive payment for an order they may be subject to change without notice in future publications or offers.

Germany

For standard deliveries within Germany we charge a shipping fee of 3,50€. Delivery is made after the confirmation to Hangload by DHL Paket within a few business days.

EU

For delivering into other EU countries we charge a shipping fee of 10€. Delivery will be handled by DHL and takes 3 to 20 weekdays.

international

For international countries we charge a shipping fee of 20€. When shipping to countries outside the EU, additional toll costs that we cannot influence may occur and must be paid by the customer. Delivery will be handled by DHL and takes 3 to 20 weekdays.
