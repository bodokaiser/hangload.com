Delivery

Germany

For standard deliveries within Germany we charge a shipping fee of 3,50€. Delivery is made after the confirmation to Hangload by DHL Paket within a few business days.
Since we are still under construction of our shipment, we ask you to excuse delays in delivery. We send you in each case an email, if we have shipped your package.

EU

For delivering into other EU countries we charge a shipping fee of 8€. Delivery will be handled by DHL and takes 3 to 20 weekdays.

international countries

For international countries we charge a shipping fee of 10€. When shipping to countries outside the EU, additional toll costs that we cannot influence may occur and must be paid by the customer. Delivery will be handled by DHL and takes 3 to 20 weekdays.
