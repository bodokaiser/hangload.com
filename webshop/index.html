product

PE retractable

The PE version is designed to be mounted on the right side of your carrier and is pulled out into position for use. This material is UV-resistant, easily recyclable, durable and robust. Although, PE is prone to marking on the surface, its color stability, robust performance and utilization are in no way affected.

- plastic: polyethylene (PE)
- metal: 100% stainless steel with perbunan tubing
-attachment: bungee cords made of polypropylene
toggle:
- 100 % beech wood
- D-Rings made of stainless steel
- webbing band from polyacryl

height: 40cm width: 40cm depth: 5cm weight: 500 g color: black

stainless steel with banner

The stainless steel version of the Hangload can be mounted on either side of your carrier - optionally on both sides simultaneously. Available in different colors.
